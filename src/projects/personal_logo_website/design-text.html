Personal logo and portfolio website, modified/created for the Fall 2017 class of ART 608 Intermediate Web Design at Kansas State University.

Personal Logo

I have been using some variant of this design since before being accepted into Graphic Design in Fall 2016. As my skills and Illustrator skills increased I would modify the design until I have what you see today.

  • Cj comes from my username of CjoewD, or Cody Joe
  • I wanted the '}' in my personal logo to represent my computer science and technological side.
  • I chose the colors of yellow and dark charcoal because yellow could represent my creative side, as well as being the color of my personality. Dark charcoal to give the logo some contrast and goes well with the yellow selected.
  • The frame was added to give the piece some structure.
  • I wanted an emphasis on the '}', thus yellow was selected as its color and the rest was to balance out the design.
