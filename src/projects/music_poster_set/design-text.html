A type experiment using three events from the SpringFest 2017 music festival, created for the Fall 2016 class of ART 300 Experimental Type at Kansas State University.

Requirements

All of the music posters must present the personality of the music of its event, yet feel as though it if from the same series.

  • All the information on the posters are required, no more and no less.
  • Personality of the music must come across the design.
  • Must feel as though it is from a set.
  • Three of the music events must be covered.
  • The posters must be square 10' by 10'.

Style Choices

  • Two colors were used for each poster, a color to fit the music personality and black.
  • I wanted the designs to play with figure/ground relationship.
  • Jazz brought in a little bit of a quirky style.
  • Trip-hop is really diverse and has that grunge and sharp look to it.
  • Classical can be seen as somewhat general, but structured.
